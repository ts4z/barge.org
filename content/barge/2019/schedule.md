The BARGE schedule has been finalized!  Mostly.  All events except ToGa (which

## BARGE 2019 Schedule

### Monday

- 6:00 pm F*** Cancer No Limit Short Deck Hold'em (with rebuys)
  ($140(100+25+15))
  - unlimited $60 rebuys during first hour &mdash; must be below starting stack
  - all rebuy proceeds to be donated to American Cancer Society

### Tuesday

- 11:00 am Triathlon of Gambling ($40 side bet for ToGa r00lers) - Details TBD
- 4:00 pm Six Handed Dealer's Choice ($140(100+25+15))
  - No-Limit Hold'em; Limit Hold'em; Razz; Seven Card Stud; Seven Card Stud
    Hi-Lo 8 or Better; Seven Card Stud Hi-Lo Regular; Pot-Limit Hold'em;
    Pot-Limit Omaha; Pot-Limit Omaha Hi-Lo 8 or Better; Pot-Limit 2-7 Lowball
    Triple Draw; Big O; Limit Omaha High; Omaha Hi-Lo 8 or Better; Limit 2-7
    Lowball Triple Draw; Ace to 5 Lowball Triple Draw; Badugi; Badeucy; Badacy;
    No-Limit 2-7 Lowball Draw; No-Limit 5-Card Draw High
- 8:00 pm Second Chance Tournament: TESLA ($80(57+15+8))
  - limit mixed game &mdash; 2-7 Lowball Triple Draw; Seven Card Stud Hi-Lo 8
    or Better; Seven Card Stud; A-5 California Lowball; Archie

### Wednesday

- 11:00 am Pot Limit HORSE ($140(100+25+15))
  - stud games will be played Mississippi-style
- 4:00 pm Second Chance Tournament: Oklahoma ($80(57+15+8))
  - limit triple board Omaha 8 or better, board(s) with lowest river card(s)
    are removed before river betting
- 7:00 pm Limit 6 Game ($140(100+25+15))
  - HORSE + Badacey

### Thursday

- 10:00 am CHORSE ($480/team (438+30+12))
- 4:00 pm Pot Limit Omaha (with lammers) ($140(100+25+15))
- 8:00 pm Second Chance Tournament: Korean ($80(57+15+8))
  - triple draw hi-lo split game with pair or better qualifier for high and no
    pair qualifier for A-5 low

### Friday

- 11:00 am Mike Sexton Original TOC-Style ($160(115+30+15))
- 5:00 pm Symposium ($40)
- 6:15 pm Speaker - Adam Pliska, President and CEO of WPT
- 7:00 pm BOD meeting
- 7:30 pm TOC restart
- 8:00 Karaoke ($25)
  - $5 discount for both Symposium & Karaoke
- 8:00 pm Second Chance Tournament: Limit Hold'Em (Win the Button)
  ($80(57+15+8))

### Saturday

- 10:30 am No Limit Hold'em Main Event ($180(135+30+15))
- 11:00 am Significant Others' Mixed Hold'em ($40(30+5+5))
- 12:00 pm Group Picture
- 5:00 pm Dinner Break
- 8:00 pm NLHE restart and Reindeer Games
