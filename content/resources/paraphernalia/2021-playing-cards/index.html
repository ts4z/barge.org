2021 Playing Cards

All the files to make a deck are available here.

In 2021, Tim Showalter and John “JZK” Scheinberg made decks to give away. Usually you just do the backs, which we got Patrick Milligan to do for us; but since the vendor had the ugliest card faces I’ve ever seen, we did our own. The card faces are standard (yet technically custom, because we did both sides), but I was really happy with the ladybug joker.

The ladybug joker and ace of spades art were done by Irene Adam.

Patrick Milligan did the card layout, the BARGE logo joker, and the Horseshoe joker. (The Horseshoe isn’t upside down; it’s the way it was depicted on Binion’s Horseshoe, the longtime home of BARGE.)
