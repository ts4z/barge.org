ATLARGE 2008 was held at the Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City from February 28 through March 2, 2008.
The TOC-style mixed event (70 entries) was won by Stephen Rosenblatt, with Tanya Peck ("MissT4") finishing second and Robert Lauria ("scsuhockey10") third. Todd Bergmann won the No Limit Hold'em event (141 entries), with David Coates finishing second and Larry Larson ("pltrgyst") third. Larson then came back to win the Seven Card Stud Hi/Lo event (39 entries) the following day, with Ross Poppel finishing second — giving Larson a remarkable two-cash weekend. MissT4 also had a strong showing, reaching the money in both the TOC event and the Stud Hi/Lo. Thursday featured the traditional smoker dinner at the Old Waterway Inn, and the Friday night banquet was again hosted by anonymous benefactors.
