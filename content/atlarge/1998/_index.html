The third ATLARGE event (ATLantic Rec.Gambling Excursion) was held April 10-12, 1998 at the Tropicana, which is well-known among poker players for its super poker room.  I think everyone had a great time, even those (like me :-) that left town with less money than they came with.  We especially want to thank Patrice Munafo, who did a great job on the trophies, the name tags, the banquet and a host of other details that kept her very busy in the weeks before the event, as well as during the event itself.  The event itself would not have been possible without the extraordinary effort of Marie McHugh, who made sure that everything was copasetic with the Casino Control Commission.  We also want to single out the tournament directors, Steve Augst and Aimee Thompson, who made sure that everything went without a hitch, and the stirling Tropicana dealers who did an amazing job, especially considering that No Limit events are pretty new at the Trop.  Of course, the extremely well-run room is the responsibility of Kate Dumas, who is doing an outstanding job.  Finally, we would be remiss if we did not mention Will Espin, Vice President of Poker and Simulcast, who has brought the Tropicana to the forefront in computer-aware poker rooms and is well-known to many of us on the net.

Attendance was down somewhat (especially on Sunday) because of the conflict with Passover and Easter, but we still had 52 in the Hold'em event and 37 in Stud.  You can check out the results from 1998's tournament, and an excellent trip report by Jeff Woods.
