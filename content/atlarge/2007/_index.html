--- date: 2007-03-05 summary: ATLARGE 2007 was held at the Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City from March 1 through March 4, 2007. tags: - atlarge - 2007 title: ATLARGE 2007 type: sequential-section --- ATLARGE 2007 was held at the Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City from March 1 through March 4, 2007. The weekend featured strong performances across all three tournaments, with Robert Lauria ("scsuhockey10") and Charles Natkins ("Chic") each making two final tables. Lauria won the H.O.E. event (79 entries), with Lauria and Natkins chopping the prize money heads-up and playing on for the trophy; Natkins finished second. Natkins then reached the final table of the No Limit Hold'em event (137 entries) again, finishing second to winner Matt Ivester. Ray DiDonato won the Seven Card Stud tournament (37 entries), with Lauria finishing second. The Friday night banquet featured writer and poker commentator Nolan Dalla as guest speaker. Thursday kicked off with the traditional smoker dinner at the Old Waterway Inn, and the $7.50/$15 pink chip hold'em game ran throughout the weekend.
