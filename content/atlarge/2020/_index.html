--- date: 2020-04-26 summary: ATLARGE 2020 was held online at devctm.com from December 11 through December 13, 2020, due to COVID-19. tags: - atlarge - 2020 title: ATLARGE 2020 type: sequential-section --- ATLARGE 2020 was originally scheduled to be held at The Borgata in Atlantic City from April 24 through April 26, 2020, but was cancelled due to COVID-19. The event was instead held online at devctm.com from December 11 through December 13, 2020.
