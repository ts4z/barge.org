What is "ATLARGE"?

ATLARGE is an annual gathering in Atlantic City of poker players who originally "met" and/or still participate on the Internet Newsgroup rec.gambling.poker (rgp). We get together in different poker friendly locations throughout the US during the year to have fun and PLAY POKER. Our biggest annual meeting is called BARGE and it is held in Las Vegas in August. ATLARGE is the Atlantic City version, and is held in early March of every year.

ATLARGE stands for: ATLantic city Annual RecGambling Excursion. Historically, the attendance at ATLARGE is second only to BARGE. In most years, from 160 to 200 r00lerz from all over the planet bring the good times to Atlantic City for four days of fun and p0cker. The most recent ATLARGE events have been held at the fabulous TRUMP TAJ MAHAL, the largest card room in Atlantic City.

Last year we played three tournaments: H.O.E., No-Limit Hold'em, and 7-Card Stud. Each even cost approximately $120, including registration fee dealer tokes. The events have often been sponsored by our friends at the fabulous TRUMP TAJ MAHAL who graciously supply our HOSPITALITY SUITE and FOOD COMPS, and PokerStars, whose generosity is rapidly becoming legendary in our group, and with good reason. Thanks to both for their continued support of the poker community and specifically our group.

If you'd like to stay informed regarding ATLARGE events and participate in ATLARGE-related discussions, join the ATLARGE mailing list at http://mail.conjelco.com/mailman/listinfo/atlarge.
