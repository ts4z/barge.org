ATLARGE 2006 was held at the Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City from March 2 through March 5, 2006.

The event again drew strong fields across three tournaments, with PokerStars sponsoring the weekend. The H.O.E. event — a nearly ten-hour marathon — was won by Jodi Neufeld, with Gabe DeVitto finishing second, Rob "Suddenly" Catlett third, and Bill Chen fourth. Joe Hammond took down the No Limit Hold'em event, with Len G in second and Jazbo Burns third out of roughly 174 entrants. John Harkness claimed the Seven Card Stud title, with Bill Chen again finishing second and Jerrod Ankenman third. The PokerStars Friday night banquet featured Tom McEvoy as a guest speaker, with Bill Chen and Jerrod Ankenman also presenting on game theory. Thursday festivities included a smoker dinner at the Old Waterway Inn and a pre-event pinball tournament at Kim Scheinberg's home. The $7.50/$15 pink chip hold'em game ran throughout the weekend.
