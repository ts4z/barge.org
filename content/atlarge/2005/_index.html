--- date: 2005-03-07 summary: ATLARGE 2005 was held at the Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City from March 3 through March 6, 2005. tags: - atlarge - 2005 title: ATLARGE 2005 type: sequential-section --- ATLARGE 2005 was held at the Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City from March 3 through March 6, 2005. The event drew nearly 200 players to the NLHE tournament, the largest field in ATLARGE history to that point, along with strong fields in the H.O.E. and Seven Card Stud events. PokerStars sponsored the weekend, picking up the rake and dealer tokes for the NLHE and hosting a private Friday night banquet. Oscar took down the NLHE, with JAB finishing second and Chrissie — a relative newcomer to tournament poker — taking third after coaching from Uncle Al. Rob "Suddenly" Catlett won the Stud tournament despite being sick for most of the weekend. The ever-popular $7.50/$15 pink chip hold'em game ran throughout the weekend alongside the tournaments.
