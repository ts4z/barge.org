ATLARGE 2023 was held at The Borgata in Atlantic City from April 21 through
April 23, 2023.

Goldie kicked off the weekend by hosting a cocktail party at his home Thursday
evening, followed by group dinners at Cafe Vola and the Knife and Fork. The
Friday TOC-style mixed event drew some commentary about the blind structure
running fast. Dean won the No Limit Hold'em event on Saturday, with Chris
Klutchman finishing second and Llew third in an exciting final table
stretch. Sunday's event was Pot Limit Omaha with a lammer add-on. Cash games
ran throughout the weekend, including 20-40 limit hold'em and 2-5 NL.
