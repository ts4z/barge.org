--- date: 2002-03-25 summary: ATLARGE 2002 was held at the Tropicana in Atlantic City from March 21 through March 24, 2002. tags: - atlarge - 2002 title: ATLARGE 2002 type: sequential-section --- ATLARGE 2002 was held at the Tropicana in Atlantic City from March 21 through March 24, 2002. Mike Keohan took down the No-Limit Hold'em tournament, with Marc Paoella finishing second and "8-2 Cactus Kev" (Kevin Suffecool) third. Bruce "Big Boy" Kramer won the Seven Card Stud event, with Glen "PubInfoGuy" Nickerson in second and Raydon third. Joan "AlwaysAware" Hadley delivered one of the weekend's most memorable tournament runs, coming back from just 55 chips to finish sixth in NLHE. The Saturday night baby pot-limit game ran until 4:30 AM and became the stuff of legend, featuring a mystery interloper dubbed "Dr. Selfish Phuc" who proceeded to run over the table for hours.
