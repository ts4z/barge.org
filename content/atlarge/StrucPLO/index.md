### Chip Races

No one will be allowed to bust out because of the chip race. Anyone in
such danger will receive one higher denomination chip.

There will be a five minute break and redraw of seats for the final
table (8 places).

All
tournaments are conducted in accordance with the rules
established by The New Jersey Casino Control Commission.
