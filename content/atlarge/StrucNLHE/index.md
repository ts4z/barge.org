## ATLARGE Event # 2 NLHE

- $100 Buy-In + $20 Entry Fee
- Initial chips: T15,000
- Rounds: All levels are 25 minutes
- 10 handed tables at start

### Chip Races
No one will be allowed to bust out because of the chip race. Anyone in
such danger will receive one higher denomination chip.

All tournaments are conducted in accordance with the rules established by
The New Jersey Casino Control Commission.
