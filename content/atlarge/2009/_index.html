--- date: 2009-03-16 summary: ATLARGE 2009 was held at the Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City from March 12 through March 15, 2009. tags: - atlarge - 2009 title: ATLARGE 2009 type: sequential-section --- ATLARGE 2009 was held at the Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City from March 12 through March 15, 2009. Travis Williams won the TOC-style mixed event, with Chris O'Connor finishing second and Sandra Benditt third. Robert Hwang won the No Limit Hold'em event, with Colleen McGraham finishing second and Ronald Lyons, Jr. third. Benditt then came back the following day to win the Stud 8 event, with Meredith Holtzman finishing second and Jazbo Burns third — giving Benditt a remarkable double-cash weekend. David Kuznick and Christine Gonzalez each cashed in two events as well. Thursday featured the traditional smoker dinner at the Old Waterway Inn, and the $7.50/$15 pink chip hold'em game ran throughout the weekend.
