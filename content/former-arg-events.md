We've hosted events all over the country, and are seeking to note them here as
their web sites have often disappeared.  We are seeking more information on
these, particularly results and trip reports.

## ESCARGOT &mdash; 1999-2006

ESCARGOT was the Experimental Southern California Recreational Gaming Outing
and Tournament, started by Lou Kreiger[^1].  Russ Fox organized several of
these.  The last ESCARGOT was in 2006.

[^1]: Colby, Anne. "[Pythagoras, Pi and
    Poker](https://www.latimes.com/archives/la-xpm-2001-may-14-cl-63164-story.html)",
    *Los Angeles Times*, May 14, 2001 12 AM PT.

If you have more details on ESCARGOT, please let us know.

## MARGE &mdash; 2001-2010

Missouri Annual Rec.Gambling Expedition, in Biloxi.  We believe Randy Collack
organized earlier events.  Mark Hughes organized later years.  Many (all?)
years appear to have been at the Beau Rivage.

MARGE events sponsored the Hope Haven charity.

If you have more details on MARGE, please let us know.
