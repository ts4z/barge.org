During the time Patrick was the designer for BCC chips, he also worked for Home Poker Chips. Home Poker Chips produced the chips for BARGE. We have some of their brochures and reference material available below.

HPC was using Chipco as a manufacturer. While Chipco’s product was well-regarded, state and federal tax authorities had issues with their non-payment of taxes. The Chipco name is now held by a new company we do not have experience with.

Many of the documents have specific comments that only apply to Chipco.
