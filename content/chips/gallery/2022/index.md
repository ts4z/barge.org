Our 2021 main event winner, the first after the Covid year, is Sabyl "KaisMom"
Landrum.  Sabyl chose the theme "Mixed Games".  Each chip depicts a different
mix:

* HORSE: [***H***old'em](/rulebook/texas-holdem.html), 
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), 
  [***R***azz](/rulebook/razz.html),
  [***S***tud](/rulebook/stud.html), and
  [Stud ***E***ight or Better](/rulebook/stud-high-low-eight-or-better.html).
  Probably the most common mixed format.
* ROSE:
  [***R***azz](/rulebook/razz.html),
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), 
  [***S***tud](/rulebook/stud.html), and
  [Stud ***E***ight or Better](/rulebook/stud-high-low-eight-or-better.html).
  HORSE without Hold'em.
* TOE:
  [2-7 ***T***riple Draw](/rulebook/deuce-to-seven-triple-draw.html),
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), 
  [Stud ***E***ight or Better](/rulebook/stud-high-low-eight-or-better.html).
* SECOR: [***S***tud](/rulebook/stud.html), 
  [Stud ***E***ight or Better](/rulebook/stud-high-low-eight-or-better.html),
  [***C***howaha](/rulebook/chowaha.html), 
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), 
  and [***R***azz](/rulebook/razz.html).  This mix was played in memory
  of Peter Secor at [EMBARGO 2026](/embargo/2026/).
* EBOLA:
  [Stud ***E***ight or Better](/rulebook/stud-high-low-eight-or-better.html),
  [***B***adugi](/rulebook/badugi.html),
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), 
  [***L***owball](/rulebook/california-lowball.html), and
  [Omah***a*** (high only)](/rulebook/omaha-high-only.html).
  This was a tournament rotation at [EMBARGO 2015](/embargo/2015/).
* SHOE:
  [***S***tud](/rulebook/stud.html), 
  [***H***old'em](/rulebook/texas-holdem.html), 
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), and
  [Stud ***E***ight or Better](/rulebook/stud-high-low-eight-or-better.html).
  A common format particularly when the WSOP was at the Horse*shoe* downtown.
* HOT:
  [***H***old'em](/rulebook/texas-holdem.html), 
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), 
  [2-7 ***T***riple Draw](/rulebook/deuce-to-seven-triple-draw.html).
* CHORSE:
  [***C***razy Pineapple](/rulebook/crazy-pineapple-high-low.html)
  [***H***old'em](/rulebook/texas-holdem.html), 
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), 
  [***R***azz](/rulebook/razz.html),
  [***S***tud](/rulebook/stud.html), and
  [Stud ***E***ight or Better](/rulebook/stud-high-low-eight-or-better.html).
  This six-person team tournament is a social highlight at every BARGE.
* BOURBON:
  [***B***adugi](/rulebook/badugi.html),
  [***O***maha (high only)](/rulebook/omaha-high-only.html), 
  [D***u***ck Flush](/rulebook/duck-flush.html),
  [***R***azz](/rulebook/razz.html),
  [***B***adacey](/rulebook/badacey.html),
  [***O***maha 8 or Better](/rulebook/omaha-high-low-eight-or-better.html), 
  [***N***o-Limit (No Limping) Deuce-to-Seven Lowball](/rulebook/deuce-to-seven-lowball.html).
* BOAT:
  [***B***adugi](/rulebook/badugi.html),
  [***O***maha (high only)](/rulebook/omaha-high-only.html), 
  [***A***rchie](/rulebook/archie.html),
  [2-7 ***T***riple Draw](/rulebook/deuce-to-seven-triple-draw.html).

These chips were designed by Patrick Milligan.
They are being produced as part of the 2026 batch.
