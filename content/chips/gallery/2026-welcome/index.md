These chips were produced as giveaways to be handed out to invite more people
to come to BARGE.

These were made in addition to the usual tournament set.

Inspired by the 2017 chips, Christian Jones suggested some more should be made.
The Reformed BARGE Chip Committee came up with several designs:

  * The **red** and **black** chips are remakes of Michael Brennan's 2017 chips,
    laid out anew by Patrick Milligan.
  * The **green** and **blue** chips were designed by Patrick Milligan and
    feature a new BARGE logo and list BARGE games on the other side.
  * The **X** and **Y** chips were designed by Tim Showalter and Patrick
    Milligan, and feature the blind squirrel from the [2025 set](../2025/).

These are being sold as part of the 2026 batch of chips.
