These chips are being produced for BARGErs to hand out to invite more people
to come to BARGE.

These are being made in addition to the usual tournament set.

Inspired by the 2017 chips, Christian Jones suggested some more should be made.
The Reformed BARGE Chip Committee came up with several designs:

  * The **red** and **black** chips are remakes of Michael Brennan's 2017 chips,
    laid out anew by Patrick Milligan.
  * The **green** and **blue** chips were designed by Patrick Milligan and
    feature a new BARGE logo and list BARGE games on the other side.
  * The **orange** and **cyan** chips were designed by Tim Showalter and
    Patrick Milligan, and feature the blind squirrel from the [2025
    set](../2025/).  The edges on these chips feature an abstract pattern and
    will differ from chip to chip.

These are being sold during the 2026 batch preorder, along with the
[2026](../2026/), [2024](../2024/), and [2022](../2022/) sample sets.
