The 2025 chip theme, chosen by 2025 Main Event winner Gillian Groves, is
*Whales*.  The chipset has eight chips, each featuring a different whale and
denomination, plus a special limited purple chip with Gillian's picture.

Chip edges feature denominations and nautical flags noting the denomination.

| Whale    | Denomination | Color        |
|:---------|:-------------|:-------------|
| Narwhal  | 1            | white        |
| Orca     | 5            | red          |
| Beluga   | 25           | green        |
| Grey     | 100          | black        |
| Right    | 500          | light purple |
| Humpback | 1000         | yellow       |
| Sperm    | 5000         | orange       |
| Blue     | 25000        | blue         |

These chips were designed by Brian Fazio, with help from the Reformed BARGE
Chip Committee.  Pictures were supplied by Gillian Groves.
